About Us

Who are we?

Podar World School is a group of schools headquartered in Mumbai. The schools lay a special emphasis on technology and innovative educational teaching methods. The group is led by Raghav Podar. The schools also consist of a preschool with the name of Podar Jumbo Kids.

In our nerul branch we have three boards- CBSE,ICSE and CIE, along with PJK.CBSE have classes from 1-10th grade.ICSE and CIE have classes from 1-12.PJK consists of playgroup,LKG and UKG.We also have many sports facilities,Labs,Music,BaskectBall classes etc.
